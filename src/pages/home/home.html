Use Summit to search for hikes in your local area based on difficulty and distance or search for details such as elevation, terrain, and current weather conditions. Track your journey as you go and use our data bank to find the closest route to find your way back onto hiking paths or to the nearest point of cellular connection. Your every hike contributes to the hiking community by collecting data and building a collection of nearest cellular connection points that makes hiking safer for all.
